Boolean Identities
  Commutative
  . x AND y = y AND x
  . x OR y = y OR x
  
  Distributive
  . x AND (y OR z) = (x AND y) or (x AND z)
  
  Associative
  . x OR (y AND z) = (x OR y) AND z
  
  DeMorgans Law = with Not gates
  . NOT(x AND y)  =  NOT (x) or Not (y)
